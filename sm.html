Soil Mechanics

    Course No. : ASCE-215

    Credit Hours: 2 (1 + 1)

    Course outline-

  • Introduction - Mechanics of soil for foundation, road construction etc., field and scope of Soil Mechanics.
  • Index properties - Phase diagram, definitions and relations, Physical and index properties of soil Particle size distribution, grain size distribution curve, soil indices. (Test)
  • Soil consistency - Plastic limit, liquid limit, shrinkage limit. (Test)
  • Stress in soil - Effective and neutral stress, Stress in soil,Boussinesq and Westerguard’s analysis, New Mark’s influence chart Stress distribution and diagrams.
  • Shear stress- Shear stress and Mohr’s stress circle, direct Shear stress, triaxial test and Vane shear test Numerical examples on different tests.
  • Compaction- Compaction of soils, Standard & modified Proctor test, Abbot’s compaction test, Jodhpur mini compaction test, field compaction methods.
  • Consolidation- Consolidation of soils, Terzaghi’s theory of one dimensional consolidation, spring analogy, Lab consolidation test, calculation of void ratio and coefficient of volume change, Taylor’s and Cassagrande method.
  • Earth pressure- Active and passive earth pressure, Rankine’s theory of earth pressure for cohesive soils.
  • Stability of slopes- Stability analysis of infinite and finite slope, Taylor’s stability number, Friction circle method.
