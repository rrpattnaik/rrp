Engineering Mechanics

    Course No. : ASCE-112

    Credit Hours: 3 (2 + 1)

    Course outline-

  • Introduction - Basic concepts, statics, dynamics, kinetics, scalar quantities, vector quantities, systems of units.
  • Composition and Resolution of forces - Characteristics of a force, system of forces, resultant of forces, composition of force, analytical method, graphical method. (Test)
  • Moments and their applications - laws of forces, moments and their application, levers, parallel forces and couples. (Test)
  • Equilibrium of forces - free body diagrams, equilibrium of forces. (Test)
  • Centre of Gravity (C.G)- Centroid: C.G. of simple geometrical figures, C.G. by moments, Plane figures, Axis of references, C.G. of symmetric sections, unsymmetrical sections, solid bodies and cut sections.
  • Moment of Inertia (M.I) - Methods of finding out M.I., methods of integration, M.I. of different sections, Theorem of perpendicular axes, parallel axes, M.I. of composite sections and cut sections.
  • Friction -Frictional forces, static friction, dynamic friction, limiting friction, normal reaction, Angle of friction, coefficient of friction, laws of friction, equilibrium of a body lying in horizontal and inclined planes, ladder friction, wedge friction, screw friction, screw jack.
  • Analysis of simple framed structure - Free body diagrams, equilibrium of forces, theory of analysis of simple framed structures: Methods of sections, force table, Methods of joints, hinged joints, roller support, vertical and inclined loads.
  • Simple stresses and strain - Hook’s law, Poisson’s ratio, modulus of elasticity, strain related problems .
  • Bending Moment and Shear force - Fundamentals of shear force and bending moment, shear force diagram (SFD) and bending moment diagram (BMD) of simply supported, cantilever and overhanging beams, point of contra-flexture.
  • Torsion of Circular shaft - Torsion, torsional effect, hoop stress, power transmitted by a shaft.
  • Principal stresses and Strain - Analysis of plane and complex stress, Principal planes and principal stresses, Mohr’s circle Finding out principal stresses, different analysis.
