Strength of Materials

    Course No. : ASCE-124

    Credit Hours: 2 (1 + 1)

    Course outline-

  • Introduction - Introduction to Strength of Materials
  • Slope and Deflection of beams - Slope and deflection of beam using integration techniques, Moment Area theorems, Conjugate beam method, Discussions on problems of slope and deflection.
  • Columns and Struts - Theory of Columns and struts, Discussions on problems of column and struts.
  • Steel Connections - Analysis of rivet connections, Analysis of Welded connections, Problems on connections
  • Stability of Masonry dam- Stability analysis of Masonry dam, Problems on Masonry dam
  • Statically Indeterminate beams - Analysis of propped beams, Analysis of Fixed beams, Analysis of continuous beams using superimposition and three moment equation.
  • Moment distribution method -Analysis of beam using Moment distribution method and solving problems.
