About Dr. R.R.Pattnaik

Dr. Rashmi Ranjan Pattnaik is a Professor of the Odisha University of Agriculture and Technology (OUAT), Bhubaneswar. He is also the PI of the Material Testing Centre of the Department of Agriculture Structure Civil and Environmental Engineering (ASCEE) of the College of Agriculture Engineering and Technology (CAET). He is serving in the University in different designations for teaching, research and administrative works of the University.
